## v2.5.0 - Performance Optimization and Economy Expansion

* **Economy Patch Stability:** Fixed issues with the economy patch causing crashes and incorrect simulation behavior.
* **Terrain and Water Optimization:** Added terrain rendering performance options and water simulation quality presets. All options can be adjusted in real-time via OptionUI during gameplay.
* **Terrain Stability:** Fixed a rare crash related to terrain rendering on large maps.
* **Economy - School and Leisure:** Added pathfinding range adjustments for school enrollment and leisure activities, adapted for large maps.
* **Economy - Vehicle AI:** Added pathfinding optimization for personal cars and taxis, reducing unnecessary long-distance routing on large maps.
* **Economy - Housing:** Added configurable household search caps to control how many families search for housing each cycle, improving simulation smoothness.
* **Economy - Shopping:** Added configurable shopping frequency and demand multiplier to fine-tune shopping traffic.
* **Pet Control:** Added a pet management tool that allows disabling pet spawning, preventing new pet generation, or removing all existing pets from the city.

---

### 主要改动

* **经济补丁稳定性：** 修复经济补丁相关的崩溃和模拟不正确问题。
* **地形与水体性能优化：** 新增地形渲染性能选项和水体模拟质量预设，所有选项均可在游戏内通过 OptionUI 实时调整。
* **地形稳定性：** 修复大型地图上地形渲染相关的罕见崩溃问题。
* **经济 - 学校与休闲：** 新增学校招生和休闲活动的寻路范围调整，适配大型地图。
* **经济 - 车辆 AI：** 新增私家车和出租车的寻路优化，减少大型地图上不必要的远距离路线计算。
* **经济 - 住房：** 新增可配置的家庭找房吞吐量上限，控制每轮搜索的家庭数量，提升模拟流畅度。
* **经济 - 购物：** 新增可配置的购物频率和需求倍率，可调节购物产生的交通量。
* **宠物管理：** 新增宠物管理工具，可禁止宠物外出、阻止新宠物生成，或一键清除城市中的所有宠物。
