## v1.2.0 - Economy Expansion

* **Economy - School and Leisure:** Added pathfinding range adjustments for school enrollment and leisure activities.
* **Economy - Vehicle AI:** Added pathfinding optimization for personal cars and taxis to reduce unnecessary routing.
* **Economy - Housing:** Added configurable household search caps to control simulation throughput.
* **Economy - Shopping:** Added configurable shopping frequency and demand multiplier to fine-tune shopping traffic.
* **Pet Control:** Added a pet management tool to disable pet spawning, prevent generation, or remove all existing pets.
* **New Settings:** Added school pathfinding cost limits, household throughput caps, and shopping behavior parameters.

---

### 主要改动

* **经济 - 学校与休闲：** 新增学校招生和休闲活动的寻路范围调整。
* **经济 - 车辆 AI：** 新增私家车和出租车的寻路优化，减少不必要的路线计算。
* **经济 - 住房：** 新增可配置的家庭找房吞吐量上限，控制模拟流畅度。
* **经济 - 购物：** 新增可配置的购物频率和需求倍率，可调节购物产生的交通量。
* **宠物管理：** 新增宠物管理工具，可禁止宠物外出、阻止生成，或一键清除所有宠物。
* **新增设置项：** 新增学校寻路费用上限、家庭处理吞吐量上限和购物行为参数。
