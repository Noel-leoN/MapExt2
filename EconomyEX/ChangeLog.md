## v2.5.5 - Land Value Balance

* **[Economy - Land Value]:** Added a "Land Value: Environment Impact Factor" slider (0-100%, default 40%) to control the transmission ratio of environmental factors (terrain attractiveness, telecom coverage, pollution) to Edge land value. This addresses the issue of excessively high commercial and industrial rents in well-developed areas caused by the mod's previous integration of environmental data into economic calculation, balancing realistic land value differentiation with rent stability.

---

### 主要改动

* **[经济 - 地价]：** 新增"地价：环境影响系数"滑块（0-100%，默认 40%），用以调节环境因子（地形吸引力、电信覆盖、污染等）向实际路网地价的传递比例。此举修复了此前因环境数据全面介入经济模拟而导致发达城区工商业租金严重偏高的问题，在保留地价真实差异化的同时维持了租金系统的平衡性。
